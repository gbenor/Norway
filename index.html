Plateaus, waterfalls, orchards, and one of Norway’s great scenic drives.

Trolltunga

The big hike: effort, weather, views, and the famous tongue of rock above Ringedalsvatnet.

08

Rosendal

A softer fjord-side pause with gardens, mountains, and quiet roads.

09

Bergen

Colorful wharves, rain-washed streets, viewpoints, and the perfect final chapter.
